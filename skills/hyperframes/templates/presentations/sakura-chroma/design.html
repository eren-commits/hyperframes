04 — Motion

Slide and
click.

Ribbons slide in from off-page on a hard ease. Cards click into position; no overshoot. Motion is mechanical, like a cassette door closing.

EASING.dll

SLIDE IN.

Aggressive head, hard stop. Use for ribbon entrances, card placements, and any element you want to feel mechanical.

cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
DURATION.dll

0.6 SEC.

Ribbons enter in 0.6s, staggered 80ms apart. Cards click in 0.3s. Hovers in 0.1s.

--dur-ribbon: 0.6s
--dur-click:  0.3s
--dur-hover:  0.1s
05 — Rules

Allowed.
Forbidden.

The catalogue stays in print the moment any of these slip.

☑ ALLOWED

YES.

  • Start cover, manifesto, and end with diagonal ribbon stripes.
  • Use Oswald 700 uppercase for every headline.
  • Place JIS-spec checkboxes under headlines for any factual list.
  • Reserve red for primary moments; blue for secondary.
  • Use Noto Sans JP for bilingual EN/JP pairs.
☒ FORBIDDEN

NO.

  • Tilt ribbons less than −12° or more than −16°.
  • Use rounded corners on any chrome.
  • Apply shadows or gradients. Print only.
  • Use sentence case in headlines.
  • Mix more than two ribbon colours per slide.
