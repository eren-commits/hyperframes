v — guidelines

Yes please.
No thank you.

A short list of rules. The garden softens the moment any are broken.

yes please.

  • Use sage and blush together — they share happily.
  • Round every corner at least 18px.
  • Outline every card in 2px ink.
  • Tilt cards ±1.5°. Never perfectly aligned.
  • Scatter daisies, stars, and rainbows freely.

no thank you.

  • Use sharp 0px corners. The page should feel kind.
  • Set type in heavy condensed sans — it fights the mood.
  • Apply harsh drop-shadows. Outlines only.
  • Use cool grey. Tint everything warm.
  • Lock cards to a strict grid.
