— Manifesto

the page is a record. the type is the voice. there is no colour because colour is not the argument.

ii — Typography

Lora and
Jost.

Lora italic carries headlines and emphasis. Jost handles body in 400 weight. JetBrains Mono runs labels. No third face.

iii — Surface

Paper, then
more paper.

No filled containers. Surfaces are defined by 1px hairlines and generous margins. The page itself is the only object.

exempla.

iv — Motion

Almost none.

The page does not move. A slide arrives by replacement, not by tween. The reader's eye does all the motion.

// transition

cut.

Replace, do not tween. The page changes when the reader is ready, not before. Fades are forbidden.

transition: none;
// duration

0s.

If any motion is required — a footnote reveal, a hover state — it happens in 150ms with a linear ease.

--dur-cut:    0s;
--dur-detail: 0.15s linear;
v — Guidelines

Sic et
non.

A handful of rules. The record breaks the moment any are violated.

sic.

  • Express emphasis through italic Lora; never through colour.
  • Keep all margins generous — 168px page padding, never less.
  • Use mono only for chrome labels and folio numbers.
  • Set body copy in Jost 400, line-height 1.75.
  • Build hierarchy with weight and size; never with hue.

non.

  • Introduce any accent colour. The system is mono.
  • Apply shadows or gradients. The page is flat.
  • Use uppercase for body. Italic carries emphasis.
  • Fill containers. Surfaces are defined by line.
  • Animate transitions. The record cuts.
