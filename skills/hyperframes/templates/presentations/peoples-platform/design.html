05 — Rules

YES.
NO.

Short rules. Break any and the message loses its voice.

YES.

  • Use one accent voice per slide — orange, navy, or red.
  • Stamp every surface with 4px black border + offset shadow.
  • Set headlines in Alfa Slab uppercase.
  • Use Caveat Brush for one human aside per slide.
  • Tilt cards ±1° — never grid-aligned.

NO.

  • Combine red and orange. They fight each other.
  • Use blurred shadows. Posters get offset shadows only.
  • Set headlines in sentence case. Posters shout.
  • Use rounded corners. Sharp edges only.
  • Hide accents. The system runs on visible position.
